You can make a tasty bread with your bread machine if you follow the recipe below.

The ingredients must be introduced in the order and quantity mentioned.

After adding each ingredient click on it (for machines with a capacity of 900 gr.).

Ingredients to add

  • Pour 330 ml of water into the bread machine
  • Add 1 teaspoon of salt (7 g)
  • Add 1 teaspoon of sugar (7 g)
  • Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil (10 ml)
  • Add 1 cup of wheat flour (550 g)
  • Add 1 tablespoon of dried yeast (7 g)
